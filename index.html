House rules

Keep it clean

  • Standard ITTF scoring — games to 11, win by 2.
  • Serve alternates every 2 points; switch ends each game.
  • Edge balls count. Arguing edge balls does not.
  • Bring your own paddle or grab a loaner at check-in.
  • Be cool. It's a company party with a scoreboard.
